Lamb Planet (2017 - 2018)

A 3D, third person sailing adventure game where you explore an archipelago, meet the residents, and help solve their problems.

I built the dialog system which allowed for branching player choice.

Synesthesia (2015 - 2016)

A 3D, third person collection-based exploration game where you collect colored sparks of light to bring a dead tree back to life.

I prototyped game mechanics, built a UI framework, and worked on level design.

Ripple (2014 - 2015)

A 2D, top down physics game where you play a lantern floating down a river trying to collect flower petals by creating ripples on the water's surface to move objects around.

Revolve (Spring 2014)

A 2D, color-matching dexterity game where you rotate a polygon to match incoming projectiles to the correct side.

Revolve was my second semester game at Digipen, built using C and an OpenGL wrapper by a team of 3.

I built the UI and wrote the math and collision libraries.

Core (Fall 2013)

A 2D, top down, twin-stick shooter set in an outer space maze where you destroy enemies, spawners, and turret projectiles with the goal of reaching and destroying the core.

Core was my first semester game at Digipen. Unfortunately I don't have a video for it, but you aren't missing much.

Built on Digipen's Zero Engine by a team of 3.
